# LinkedIn Post Draft

I open-sourced FEA Copilot as a small but opinionated repository for reproducible first-pass structural checks.

The project focuses on a narrow regime: linear-elastic beams and rectangular plates, with explicit analytical screening examples for brackets and plates with holes. The repository now includes committed examples, validation cases, mesh-convergence evidence, contributor scaffolds, and agent-readable documentation.

The intent is not to replace full engineering review. It is to make assumptions, run commands, and result bundles easier to inspect and reuse.
